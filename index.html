Alpha and Omega Glass Company is opening its doors in Indianapolis with a simple promise: glass work done right, from the first measurement to the final seal. Originally from Texas, our owners bring more than 25 years of hands-on experience to every project.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

What areas do you serve?

We're launching in Indianapolis, IN and serving the surrounding areas. Our family's roots and 25+ years of experience come from Texas.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Every quote is free and comes with no obligation. Send us your project details and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Do you handle both residential and commercial work?

Absolutely. From home windows, mirrors and custom showers to commercial glass, aluminum storefronts, curtain walls, door closers and thresholds, we cover the full range.

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes, we're fully bilingual. You can work with us in English or Spanish at every step, from the first call to the finished install.

We reply to every quote request within 24 hours. For urgent repairs or broken glass, call us directly and we'll do our best to help right away.
